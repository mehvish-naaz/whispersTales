About Whisper Tales

Welcome to Whisper Tales, a cozy corner for anyone who loves to get lost in a good story. I’m Mehvish Naaz Khan, a passionate storylover and web developer exploring the magic of short stories through this platform.

This website is a curated collection of tales across fantasy, fiction, horror, romance, mystery, and thrillers and more, designed to bring stories to your screen in a simple and enjoyable way. Whether you’re looking for a quick escape or a story to inspire, you’ll find something here to spark your imagination.

You can browse stories by genre, search for your favorites, or discover hidden gems you’ve never encountered before. My goal is to make reading accessible, fun, and interactive, all while improving my skills as a web developer.

I hope you enjoy exploring the stories as much as I enjoy sharing them. Feel free to reach out if you have feedback, story suggestions, or just want to say hi—your thoughts are always welcome to contact me.

Public Domain Note

Many of the stories on this site are from the public domain. This means they are no longer under copyright, and you are free to read, share, and enjoy them without restriction. Sources like Project Gutenberg make these classic tales accessible to everyone.
